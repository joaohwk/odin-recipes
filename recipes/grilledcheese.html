Grilled Cheese

Grilled cheese is a simple yet satisfying comfort food that consists of melted cheese sandwiched between two slices of bread that have been grilled or toasted to a crispy golden-brown. It is a popular dish enjoyed by people of all ages, and can be customized by using different types of cheese, bread, and additional ingredients such as bacon or tomato. Grilled cheese is easy to make and can be enjoyed as a quick snack or a filling meal.

Ingredients

  • White bread
  • Sliced Cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Take two slices of bread and spread butter on one side of each slice.
  2. Place a slice of cheese on the unbuttered side of one slice of bread.
  3. Place the other slice of bread, buttered side up, on top of the cheese.
  4. Heat a nonstick pan over medium heat and place the sandwich in the pan.
  5. Cook for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until the bread is golden brown and the cheese has melted.
  6. Remove from the pan and cut the sandwich in half, then serve hot.
