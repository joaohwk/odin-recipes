Pesto Pasta

Pesto pasta is a flavorful and easy-to-prepare dish made by tossing cooked pasta with a rich and creamy sauce made from fresh basil, garlic, pine nuts, Parmesan cheese, and olive oil. The pesto sauce is blended until smooth, creating a vibrant green sauce that coats each strand of pasta with its rich and savory flavor. Pesto pasta can be served as a side dish or as a main course, and can be customized by adding additional ingredients such as cherry tomatoes, grilled chicken, or sautéed vegetables. Pesto pasta is a delicious and satisfying meal that can be prepared quickly and easily for a weeknight dinner or a special occasion.

Ingredients

  • Your favorite pasta shape
  • Onion
  • Oil
  • Pesto
  • Seasonings
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook pasta in a pot of salted boiling water until al dente, then drain and set aside.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, chop a small onion and sauté it in a pan with a little oil until it is softened.
  3. Add pesto to the pan and stir to combine with the onion. Heat for a minute or two until the sauce is warmed through.
  4. Add the cooked pasta to the pan and toss it with the pesto sauce until the pasta is fully coated.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Top the pasta with grated Parmesan cheese and serve hot.
